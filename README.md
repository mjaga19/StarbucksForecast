# Forecasting Starbucks Growth: Trends, Insights, and Projections
**Objective:**

To analyse the Starbucks company sales revenue dashboards for the FY 2018 -2024 and provide sales forecast for Year 2025


**Overview:**
1.	A **5-month moving average (5MA)** symmetrical smoothing technique is applied to generate the initial sales forecast.
2.	Forecast visualizations are developed for all three business segments:
     *     Line of Business (LOB): Beverages, Food, Other
     *     Market: North America, International, Other
     *     Store Type: Company-operated, Licensed, Other
       
4.	Year-over-year **(YoY%)** growth is calculated for each category within the above segments.
5.	Key observations and data-driven recommendations are provided based on the analysis.
